Workshops

I also work closely with a colleague, Kirsty Knight. Together we make up Salus Training, offering workshops for adults and children on a variety of mental health issues.

Previous workshops have included:

  • ‘Beat Your Anxiety’ workshops for adults
  • Anxiety workshops for children
  • Sessions for parents to support their children
  • Calming, Sensory workshops

We also offer bespoke sessions for schools, aimed at young people and educational professionals, as well as workshops for companies to support their employees.
